Herbs

Herbs are small, non-woody plants that usually have a soft and green stem. They are often used in cooking and medicine for their aromatic or medicinal properties, like basil or mint.

Shurbs

Shrubs are medium-sized, woody plants with multiple stems that grow close to the ground. Examples include hibiscus and lavender, often used for decorative purposes in gardens.

Trees

Trees are large, woody plants with a single main stem or trunk that supports branches and leaves. They can live for many years, providing shade and producing fruit, such as oak or apple trees.
